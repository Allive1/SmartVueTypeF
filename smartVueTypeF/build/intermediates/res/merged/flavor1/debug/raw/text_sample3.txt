 Crossfade animations (also know as dissolve) gradually fade out one UI component 
 while simultaneously fading in another. This animation is useful for situations 
 where you want to switch content or views in your app. Crossfades are very subtle 
 and short but offer a fluid transition from one screen to the next. When you 
 don't use them, however, transitions often feel abrupt or hurried.
